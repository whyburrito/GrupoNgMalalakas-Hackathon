AnonyMask Studio

We believe that the masks we wear to survive the world shouldn't have to be worn in private. ANONYMASK was born at the intersection of clinical data and raw human expression. Most mental health tools feel like a lecture; we wanted a studio. We built this platform for the outliers, the artists, and the thinkers who find traditional "mood tracking" too shallow and social media too performative.
